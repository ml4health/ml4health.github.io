Become a Sponsor

Financial support ensures that ML4H remains accessible to a broad set of participants by offsetting the expenses involved in participation. We follow best practices in other conferences to maintain a transparent and appropriate relationship with our funders:

  1. The substance and structure of the symposium are determined independently by the program committees.
  2. All papers are chosen through a rigorous, mutually anonymous peer review process, where authors disclose conflicts of interest.
  3. All sources of financial support are acknowledged.
  4. Benefits are publicly disclosed below.
  5. Corporate sponsors cannot specify how contributions are spent.

2023 Sponsorship Levels

Sponsorship of the Machine Learning for Health (ML4H) symposium contributes to furthering research and interdisciplinary dialogue around machine learning, health, and biomedicine. We deeply appreciate any amount of support your company or foundation can provide.

Presenting Sponsor [1] ($25,000 USD)

  • Company name listed as “ML4H presented by [company]” on website header
  • Exclusive acknowledgment to attendees before start of lunch break
  • Dedicated table throughout the conference
  • Present a 20-minute lunch talk on company's work in machine learning and health research or development
  • Display of company logo on website, badges, and pamphlets
  • Access to attendee information such as name, email, job title, of those who opt-in for career opportunities
  • Verbal acknowledgment of contribution in the opening and closing remarks of the conference
  • Free registration for up to 10 representatives from your organization
