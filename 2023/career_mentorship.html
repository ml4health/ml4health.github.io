The full original call for the career mentorship program is below.

The Career Mentorship Program will take place remotely throughout the week before the symposium (November 7, 2023 – December 1, 2023), and will focus on pairing mentees with mentors who can provide advice on career-related topics (e.g., developing a long-term research plan, doing healthcare research in industry, work-life balance). The primary expectations of the program are:

  1. Attendance of an online one-hour group mentoring workshop on November 21, 2023;
  2. Participation in an hour-long 1-1 mentoring session to provide advice on the topics of interest.

We especially encourage less-experienced attendees to sign up as mentees, as well as senior community members to serve as mentors for these programs.

If you are interested in participating in the career mentorship program as a mentor or mentee, please sign up using the following forms:

  • Sign up as a mentor: https://forms.gle/VZAU9G4Lq8urBsHR8
  • Sign up as a mentee: https://forms.gle/xpAzczgHvAfk3drE7
  • Deadline: October 15 AoE, 2023.

Mentors and mentees will be matched based on time availability and selected topics of expertise/interest for providing and receiving mentorship respectively. We will aim to match each mentor with 3-4 mentees.

We aim to gather a diverse pool of mentees and mentors. Please share broadly and do not hesitate to sign up if you are from an under-represented group in the field!

Mentees are expected to:

  • Prepare questions and topics of discussion with the mentors.
  • Participate in the ML4H career mentorship program.
  • Abide by the Code of Conduct.

Mentors are expected to:

  • Read the resources provided for mentors.
  • Participate in the ML4H career mentorship program.
  • Abide by the Code of Conduct.

Timeline

  • October 15, 2023: Deadline for applying to participate in the ML4H Career Mentorship Program.
  • November 1, 2023: Mentor-mentee pairings are sent out.
  • November 2, 2023 – November 10, 2023: Mentees reach out to mentors to schedule a zoom call for the week before ML4H.
  • November 15, 2023: Lists of mentors to mentees and on the ML4H website.
  • November 21, 2023: Group Mentoring session mentors and mentees can choose the session to participate in based on their availability and are welcome to join both sessions.
  • November 27, 2023 – December 1, 2023: Mentors and mentees meet for 1-1 discussion.

Code of Conduct

By participating in the Career Mentorship Program, we expect mentors and mentees to abide by the ML4H Code of Conduct. To report any violations of the Code of Conduct, please contact info@ml4h.cc.
