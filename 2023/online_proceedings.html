Online Proceedings

Since 2019, Machine Learning for Health has held two submission tracks: a full, archival proceedings track and a non-archival, extended abstract track. The proceedings of ML4H are published as volumes of the Proceedings of Machine Learning Research series.

Proceedings Track:

  • Proceedings of the 3rd Machine Learning for Health symposium 2023 (ML4H 2023)
  • Proceedings of the 2nd Machine Learning for Health symposium 2022 (ML4H 2022), PMLR volume 193, Issn: 2640-3498
  • Proceedings of 1st Machine Learning for Health symposium 2021 (ML4H 2021), PMLR volume 158, Issn: 2640-3498
  • Proceedings of the 6th Machine Learning for Health workshop 2020 (ML4H 2020), PMLR volume 136, Issn: 2640-3498
  • Proceedings of the 5th Machine Learning for Health workshop 2019 (ML4H 2019), PMLR volume 116, Issn: 2640-3498

Extended Abstract Track:

  • Collection of the accepted abstracts for the ML4H Abstract Track 2022
  • Collection of the accepted abstracts for the ML4H Abstract Track 2021
  • Collection of the accepted abstracts for the ML4H Abstract Track 2020
  • Collection of the accepted abstracts for the ML4H Abstract Track 2019
  • Collection of the accepted submissions to ML4H 2018
  • Collection of the accepted submissions to ML4H 2017
  • Program of ML4H 2016, NeurIPS 2016 Workshop book (pp. 20-22)
